BLISS is a Bitcoin Cash conference to celebrate the Bitcoin Cash upgrade to [ABLA](/faqs/BCH/what-is-the-maximum-bch-blocksize). It is being held in Ljubljana, Slovenia on May 14th & 15th.

[Official conference website](https://www.bliss.cash/).

Jonathan Siverblood is the main organiser, with help from [Jeremy](/about) & Ryan Giffin.

## Tickets

Tickets to the event come in the form of a limited edition of 100 unique Jessica [CashToken](/faqs/Tech/what-is-cashtokens) NFTs.
