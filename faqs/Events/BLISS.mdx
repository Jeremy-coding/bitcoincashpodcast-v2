BLISS was a Bitcoin Cash conference to celebrate the Bitcoin Cash upgrade to [ABLA](/faqs/BCH/what-is-the-maximum-bch-blocksize). It was held in Ljubljana, Slovenia on May 14th & 15th 2024.

[Official conference website](https://www.bliss.cash/).

Jonathan Siverblood was the main organiser, with help from [Jeremy](/about) & Ryan Giffin.

## Livestream

Recorded portions of the event include a pre-show episode of The Bitcoin Cash Podcast, 9 presentations from industry speakers & a closing panel discussion plus some additional interviews.
