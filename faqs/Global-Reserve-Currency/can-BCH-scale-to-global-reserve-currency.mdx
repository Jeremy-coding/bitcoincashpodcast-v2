title: Can Bitcoin Cash technically scale to global reserve currency?
---

Yes.

Satoshi Nakamoto's design in [the Bitcoin whitepaper](/bitcoin.pdf), plus ongoing innovation in underlying computer hardware allows Bitcoin Cash to scale "Layer 1" to the entire world. [Billions of users can transact 5 times per day](/faqs/Global-Reserve-Currency/what-do-you-mean-global-reserve-currency) - on-chain & non-custodially - without unduly compromising node decentralisation. This was the [original mission & plan](/faqs/BCH-vs-BTC/is-bch-the-real-bitcoin) for Bitcoin.
