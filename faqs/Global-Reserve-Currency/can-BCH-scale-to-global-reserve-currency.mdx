title: Can Bitcoin Cash technically scale to global reserve currency?
---

Yes. Satoshi Nakamoto's design in [the Bitcoin whitepaper](/bitcoin.pdf), plus ongoing innovation in underlying computer hardware allows Bitcoin Cash to scale "Layer 1" to 10 billion users transaction 5 times per day - on-chain & non-custodially. This was the original mission & plan for Bitcoin.
