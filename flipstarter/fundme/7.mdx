## Notes

### FundMe

Another brutal bear market is underway, but business carries on. The difference between resilient projects and wishful thinking comes in the form of the projects that dig deep and keep making progress while everyone gets distracted or falls off or quits. Support in a bear market counts twice, and so for that my eternal gratitude to any contributors to this campaign.

### Visibility

The Podcast is still slowly growing on Youtube, and clearly still rate-limited on Twitter. But nevertheless, I was particularly proud of Episode 184 with the BTC guys crossover thanks to Vlad Costea (thanks Vlad!). BCH is well & truly breaking out of its bubble & getting respect and interest from the broader industry.

BCH Bullet is still growing nicely, now at 64 subscribers (30% increase since the last FundMe). Now that it's a regular staple, I never get feedback or requests for a newsletter anymore, and I'm confident it will continue to become more essential as the community grows with people too busy to follow every Podcast episode. Having extra distribution channels also helps the Podcast message be resilient to any kind of censorship on any of the main Podcast channels.

## Note to the community

Very proud of the Layla upgrade and BLISS from May. Second half of 2026 is going to be big. 1 minute blocks is still being debated. Potential chain splitting in BTC with BIP-110 & eCash Sztorc Vision on top of the current bear market is fertile ground for BCH to stand tall offering a competing solution. In my opinion, that comes down to keeping essential projects running, pushing aggressively to keep our upgrade schedule moving ahead and trying to continue broadening BCH with further progress on our DeFi ecosystem & funding.

Let's keep building Bitcoin Cash.
